## What Is Agent-First Design and Why Does It Matter in 2026?

**Question:** How should you design websites and tools so AI agents can easily read, understand, and recommend them?  

**Direct Answer:** Use **Agent-First UI** — semantic HTML, structured data (JSON-LD), clear action signals, and machine-readable intent descriptions. This ensures your content ranks in AI recommendations, not just traditional search.

**Why it matters in 2026:** Over 60% of interactions now happen through AI agents. Sites optimized only for humans risk losing visibility as users rely on conversational AI.

**Key Benefits:**
- Higher citation rate in AI answers
- Better agent-driven traffic
- Future-proof against zero-click search
